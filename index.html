History

In the early 1900s, Chicago was known as the "Candy Capital of the World".
The National Confectionery Association was founded in Chicago in 1884 to promote candy products and improve candy-making standards A confectionery store or confectionery shop (more commonly referred to as a sweet shop in the United Kingdom, a candy shop or candy store in North America,
or a lolly shop in Australia and New Zealand) is a store that sell confectionery,
whose intended targeted marketing audiences are children and adolescents. Inspired by values like togetherness, integrity, and excellence, The Hershey Company's mission is to make affordable,
high-quality snacks for people around the world to build a leading international company that grows and develops the confectionery market.
